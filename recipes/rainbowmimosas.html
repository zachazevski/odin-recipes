Odin Rainbow Mimosas recipe

Description

Just try it.

Ingridients

  • 5 glasses Champagne
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine
  • Crushed ice
  • 10 tablespoons pineapple juice (or any fruit juice)
  • 60ml gin (or any clear liquor)
  • Splash curacao

Steps

  1. Line up five glasses of Champagne on a work surface.
  2. To a cocktail shaker, add the grenadine and enough crushed ice to fill by three-quarters. Slowly pour in the pineapple juice over the back of a spoon or ice cream scoop. Then add the gin and curacao; this will create layers. Strain into the Champagne glasses.
